Created an autonomous system capable of detecting, strategizing motion, and collecting an allotted amount of metal objects in the shortest period of time possible. Devised a self-driving robot utilizing an electromagnetic servo assembly. Programmed firmware in C and Python to debug, test and plan optimal motion patterns for efficient coin collection.
